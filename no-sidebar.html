Poster Presentation

"Design and Performance Analyses of DC-DC and DC-AC Converters with Optimization of Different Performance Parameters," showcases a sophisticated study into optimizing converter efficiency. This work focuses on improving renewable energy systems by enhancing both DC-AC inverters and DC-DC converters. By integrating a Buck-Boost converter and reducing THD in inverters, your research offers impactful solutions for hybrid renewable systems, ensuring more stable voltage levels and boosting overall system performance. The clear methodology, detailed circuit diagrams, and comprehensive results make this an excellent demonstration of your expertise in power electronics and renewable technologies.
