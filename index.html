About Me

I am Shafiq Rahman, an Electrical and Electronic Engineering graduate from Ahsanullah University of Science and Technology (AUST), Bangladesh, with a passion for cutting-edge technology and innovative solutions. My research interests span VLSI design, machine learning, artificial intelligence, robotics, renewable energy, and power electronics. I aim to contribute to solving real-world problems through engineering solutions that are both sustainable and efficient. With hands-on experience in circuit design, digital logic design, and machine learning, I have developed technical expertise in C/C++, Python, MATLAB, Simulink, Verilog, and Cadence. I am particularly focused on VLSI and its applications in creating low-power, high-efficiency systems for IoT and other advanced technologies.
